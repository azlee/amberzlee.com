About Me

My Story

When I first entered university, I was enrolled in Biomedical Science with a plan to pursue Pharmacy. However while studying sciences at the university level, I never really felt the fulfillment I wanted. Going to class and labs and completing assignments felt like a chore at the time. And after volunteering at a pharmacy, I knew that it wasn't my true passion. So I took a chance early this year and switched to Math since I knew I had a strong logical mind. And after taking computer science courses, I found a passion for coding! I never had any prior experience with coding, however I really enjoy the challenges and problem-solving aspect of it. Going to classes everyday and learning new languages is something I look forward to now and I find it to be so rewarding. I feel incredibly fortunate to have found something that I love learning! I am now pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science.

Of course, I still have a lot to learn when it comes to coding and web & app developement. But I am excited to continue building on my knowledge and I'm always searching for more learning opportunities!
